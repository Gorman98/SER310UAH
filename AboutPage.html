About Us

Looking on becoming a superhero. Look no further because UA High is the perfect fit for you! We believe in helping you to reach you best hero potential. Located in Musutafu, Japan, UA High Is the #1 high school for heroes.

We believe in a well-diversified student and house multiple departments to meet the need of the student! Some of our departments include the Department of Heroes, General Education, Support, and Management. In addition, we house multiple classes organized from A to K.

In the Department of Heroes students learn how to be heroes and in the community and public spaces. In the Department of Education, heroes receive excellent classes and are prepared for college, top students are moved into the Department of heroes. The Department of Support get to work with tools that will help heroes in battle. Lastly, our Department of Management evokes the entrepreneurial spirit in helping students found hero agencies.

Enrolling in UA High is a great place to become a hero! We have the world-class resources, staff, and a safe environment. Feel free to come in for a visit or call (123) - 456 - 7899 to learn more!.
